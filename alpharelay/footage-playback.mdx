Uploaded clips appear under **Mission Footage**. From there, operators can:

- Watch footage using the built-in video player
- Play, pause, and scrub through clips
- Save a copy using the browser’s normal save or download options
- Open the clip with timeline events using **Play with Events**

There is a separate download button on each clip.

## Play footage with events

Use **Play with Events** to open the timeline view with event markers, synced data, mission timestamps, and related logged activity.

After the mission is closed, use **Play with Events** in the sticky header for that past mission.

## Evidence controls

All AlphaRelay missions include evidence controls for managing mission footage and maintaining a clear review record.

In the **Mission Footage** area, operators can use:

- Custody and integrity controls
- Cloud evidence controls
- Primary footage selection
- Multi-angle sync tools

These tools help teams organize footage, identify the primary video source, align multiple angles, and support a clear chain-of-custody workflow for mission review and reporting.
