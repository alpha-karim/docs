## Open AlphaRelay in the browser

Go to [https://www.alpha-relay.com](https://www.alpha-relay.com), then sign in using your operator credentials.

When a mission appears under **Active missions**, click **Open in console** to open Mission Overwatch for that flight. Sign in once from the Mission Dashboard homepage if you landed there without a focused mission yet. Mission Overwatch can also be installed as a PWA from the **Install app** button when the browser supports it, so the static UI loads again on the next visit even without internet (cloud sign-in and sync still need connectivity).

## Install the AlphaRelay pilot app

Download the Android APK from [https://www.alpha-relay.com/download.html](https://www.alpha-relay.com/download.html). The pilot app runs on approved Android tablets or phones — use whichever form factor your team has standardized on.

After downloading:

1. Install and open the app.
2. Grant the requested permissions, including camera, microphone, storage, location, and any other required access.
3. Step through the **First-Time Guide** that auto-opens on first launch.
4. Activate the pilot license when prompted.

To activate the license:

1. In AlphaRelay, go to **Organization** → **Pilot tablet/phone (Android bridge) license**.
2. Use the QR code or license key issued by your admin.
3. Scan or enter the license inside the pilot app when prompted. The first time you tap **Choose Mission Mode**, the app asks for a license if none is saved.

You can also update the license later from **Pilot console → AlphaRelay license**, or by long-pressing **Choose Mission Mode** on the main screen.

The pilot app validates license keys against AlphaRelay and can continue within its offline validation window after a recent successful check. If the validation window expires, connect the tablet or phone to the internet before starting the relay again.

## Set up account and organization details

Before field use, open **Account** and save your full name and rank or title. These values can appear in member lists, mission attribution, and custody logs.

Organization owners or admins should also open **Organization** to confirm team membership, roles, organization time zone, storage summary, and pilot device license visibility.
