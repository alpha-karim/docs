description: "Recap of the full AlphaRelay mission lifecycle from setup through offline export."

---

AlphaRelay’s lifecycle is intentionally linear: **setup → fly (LAN relay and/or remote live) → log → close → sync footage/photos → review in Play with events → Alpha AI report → custody seal → supervisor approval → optional offline ZIP.**

Teams capture timestamped events (pilot, overwatch, voice relay, optional local AI, post-mission tags), attach screenshots and mission photos, defer uploads when the network is poor, align secondary angles to primary footage, then freeze custody and approve the report when command is satisfied.

Supervisor **Approve report** locks the narrative and timeline permanently; **Export offline package…** captures a point-in-time archive for retention or handoff.
