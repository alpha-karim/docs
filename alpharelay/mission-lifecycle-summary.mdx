With AlphaRelay, operators can move through the full mission lifecycle in one connected workflow:

**Setup → Live relay → Flight → Structured logging → Closeout → Footage upload or queue → Multi-angle event review → Post-mission tagging → Alpha AI report generation → Officer review → Evidence sealing → Supervisor approval → Offline export**

AlphaRelay helps teams capture timestamped events, preserve mission context, attach screenshots, upload or locally queue footage, review synchronized multi-angle evidence, tag additional events from post-mission video frames, and generate structured after-action reports with Alpha AI.

Before final submission, officers can address any **Items Requiring Officer Review**, edit the report, confirm the mission timeline, verify the evidence record, and seal the mission to prevent further evidence changes.

After the mission is complete, teams can also export an offline mission package for records retention, handoff, command review, archival storage, or offline access. This package gives the team a self-contained copy of the mission record, including the report, custody data, certificate, timeline, event screenshots, and available media.

The result is a complete, review-ready mission package built for operational documentation, chain-of-custody support, supervisor approval, offline review, and long-term records.
