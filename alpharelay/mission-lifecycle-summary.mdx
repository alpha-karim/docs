## What this is

This reference summarizes how live capture becomes a reviewed report and mission package.

## When to use it

- You need the full workflow at a glance.
- You are training supervisors or reviewers.
- You need to explain how mission records move from field capture to export.

## Basic steps

1. Set up devices, accounts, licenses, and roles.
2. Start Field Mission or Relay Mission from the Android field device.
3. Log field and Mission Overwatch events as needed.
4. End the mission.
5. Upload footage or let queued footage sync.
6. Review footage, photos, and timeline events.
7. Generate and review the Alpha AI report draft.
8. Resolve Items Requiring Officer Review.
9. Seal custody evidence when ready.
10. Submit and approve the report when appropriate.
11. Export the offline package.

## What good looks like

- Mission events and media are reviewed before reporting.
- The report is treated as draft material until human review is complete.
- Evidence sealing and report approval are handled as separate steps.
- The offline package reflects the reviewed mission state at export time.

## Common problems

- Report generation is treated as final approval.
- Evidence sealing is confused with supervisor approval.
- Export happens before upload or sync completes.

## More detail

| Stage | Main responsibility | Result |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Setup | Field operator and Mission Overwatch user | Devices, roles, licenses, and network path are ready. |
| Live mission | Field operator and Mission Overwatch user | Events, screenshots, telemetry, and notes are captured. |
| Closeout and upload | Field operator or reviewer | Footage is uploaded or queued for later sync. |
| Review | Reviewer or officer | Timeline, footage, photos, and post-mission tags are checked. |
| Report draft | Reviewer or officer | Alpha AI draft material is reviewed and edited. |
| Evidence sealing | Officer or authorized reviewer | Custody evidence and hashes are preserved. |
| Supervisor approval | Supervisor | Final report is locked. |
| Offline export | Reviewer, supervisor, or records staff | Point-in-time package is created for handoff or archive. |
