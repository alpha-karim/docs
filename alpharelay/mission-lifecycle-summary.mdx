This page is for supervisors, reviewers, and trainers who need the whole mission lifecycle at a glance. Use it to explain how live capture becomes a reviewed report and evidence package.

With AlphaRelay, operators can move through the full mission lifecycle in one connected workflow:

**Setup → Live relay or remote live → Flight → Structured logging → Closeout → Footage upload or queue → Mission photo upload → Multi-angle event review → Post-mission tagging → Alpha AI report generation → Officer review → Evidence sealing → Supervisor approval → Offline export**

AlphaRelay helps teams capture timestamped events, preserve mission context, attach screenshots and photos, upload footage or locally queue pilot handoffs, review synchronized multi-angle evidence, tag additional events from post-mission video frames, and generate structured after-action reports with Alpha AI.

Before final submission, officers can address any **Items Requiring Officer Review**, edit the generated report narrative, confirm the mission timeline, verify the evidence record, and seal registered custody evidence. Supervisor approval is the final step that makes the report permanent and locks timeline events and photos.

After the mission is complete, teams can also export an offline mission package for records retention, handoff, command review, archival storage, or offline access. This package gives the team a self-contained copy of the mission record, including the report, custody data, certificate, timeline, event screenshots, and available media.

| Stage | Main responsibility | Result |
|-------|---------------------|--------|
| Setup | Field operator and Mission Overwatch operator | Devices, roles, licenses, and network path are ready. |
| Live mission | Field operator and Mission Overwatch operator | Events, screenshots, telemetry, and live notes are captured. |
| Closeout and upload | Field operator or reviewer | Footage is uploaded or queued for later sync. |
| Review | Reviewer or officer | Timeline, footage, photos, and post-mission tags are checked. |
| Report draft | Reviewer or officer | Alpha AI draft material is reviewed and edited. |
| Evidence sealing | Officer or authorized reviewer | Custody evidence and hashes are preserved. |
| Supervisor approval | Supervisor | Final report is locked. |
| Offline export | Reviewer, supervisor, or records staff | Point-in-time package is created for handoff or archive. |

The result is a complete, review-ready mission package built for operational documentation, chain-of-custody support, supervisor approval, offline review, and long-term records.
