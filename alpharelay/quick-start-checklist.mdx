Short path for a standard **full relay** test. Each bullet is roughly one checkpoint. The pilot app runs on approved Android tablets or phones.

## Before you fly (once)

- Sign in at [alpha-relay.com](https://www.alpha-relay.com); save **name** and **rank** under **Account**.
- Install the pilot app from [alpha-relay.com/download.html](https://www.alpha-relay.com/download.html) and activate your license (QR or `ar-…` key from **Organization**).

## Live test (minimal)

1. Power on drone and RC; plug the pilot device into the RC; put the pilot device and command laptop on the **same Wi‑Fi**.
2. Pilot app: **Choose Mission Mode** → pick scenario (optional) → **Full relay**.
3. Command browser: open the **dashboard**, click **Open in console** on the active mission (Mission Overwatch opens for that mission). If prompted, trust the relay certificate once; **Connect / Scan LAN** are only fallback tools if automatic relay connection does not come up.

## Logging and shutdown

- Log one quick event from the pilot and one from Mission Overwatch, then fly briefly per DJI rules, **Land**, and tap **Stop Mission** on the pilot.

## Wrap-up (optional smoke test)

- After sync, open **Play with events** (confirm markers), optionally **Generate Report** and **Export Offline Package**.

For detail on networks, streaming, or **pilot-only** missions, continue with [Mission setup](/alpharelay/mission-setup) and [Pilot app and local AI](/alpharelay/pilot-app-and-local-ai).
