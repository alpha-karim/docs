## Goal

Run a short training mission and confirm AlphaRelay captures mission activity, events, footage, and a report.

## Before you start

- Android field device is ready.
- Mission Overwatch user is signed in.
- Devices are prepared for the selected mission type.
- The organization, users, and device access are already set up.
- Internet access is available when you need cloud sync, upload, reporting, or export.

## Steps

1. Open the Android field app.
2. Choose the mission type.
3. Start the mission.
4. Open the mission in Mission Overwatch if using Relay Mission.
5. Add one test event from the field device.
6. Add one test event from Mission Overwatch if that workflow is being used.
7. End the mission.
8. Confirm footage and events are available or queued according to the documented workflow.
9. Review the mission timeline.
10. Generate a test report.
11. Seal and export the final record if that is part of your current workflow.

## You are successful when

- The mission appears in the dashboard.
- Events appear on the timeline.
- Footage is available or queued according to the documented workflow.
- A report can be generated.
- The final record can be sealed and exported when supported by the workflow.

## Common problems

- Mission Overwatch cannot connect because Relay Mission devices are not on the same reachable local network.
- Footage is still queued and needs internet before final review.
- The report needs officer review before submission or approval.
