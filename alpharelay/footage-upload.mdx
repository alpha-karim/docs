## Auto upload or manual upload

After the mission, the pilot app may attempt to automatically upload the recorded footage.

For auto upload to work, the pilot tablet must be connected to a network with internet access. Automatic upload can take time and may continue using the drone, controller, or tablet battery while the file is transferring.

If you do not want to wait for auto upload, or if battery life is a concern, tap **Skip**, then manually upload the footage later.

When the pilot app has mission video ready after landing or stopping the relay, it shows a footage upload banner. The banner can show upload progress, the number of mission videos waiting locally, and an **Upload now** action when the tablet can reach the cloud.

For supported aircraft workflows, the pilot app may start SD recording after takeoff and stop recording after a successful land command. If no SD clip is pulled, record with DJI during flight, check the aircraft storage path, or upload the MP4 manually from Mission Overwatch.

**To manually upload the file**

1. Remove the SD card from the aircraft or connect the aircraft to a computer.
2. Copy the mission MP4 file to your computer.
3. Open the mission in AlphaRelay.
4. Go to **Mission Footage**.
5. Click **Upload MP4**.
6. Select the correct video file.

Use auto upload when you have a reliable internet-connected network and enough battery remaining. Use manual upload when you need to preserve battery, leave the field quickly, or upload the file from a computer later.

## Deferred local upload

If AlphaRelay receives a mission video but cannot reach the cloud, it can keep the file locally and upload it automatically later. This can happen when:

- The pilot tablet sends video over the local relay network but the Mission Console does not have internet access
- A manual browser upload starts while cloud connectivity is unavailable
- A cloud upload begins but fails before the file is saved

Stay signed in and bring the device back online when possible. AlphaRelay retries queued footage automatically, and the pilot tablet may also offer **Upload now** when pending mission videos are available.

If you see **Queued for cloud sync**, **Loading playback link**, or a pending-video count, keep AlphaRelay open and online until the upload and playback link finish.

## Mission photos

Mission Overwatch also supports mission photo uploads.

Use **Mission photos** to upload images that should travel with the mission record. During upload, choose whether the photos are:

- **Timeline photos**, tied to a mission time
- **Evidence-only photos**, stored with the mission but not inserted into the event timeline

You can add a caption, category, notes, and timeline placement. Mission photos appear in the mission timeline, report, custody views, storage summary, and offline package when available.
