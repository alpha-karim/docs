AlphaRelay turns a field mission into a clear, reviewable record.

During a mission, the Android field device captures mission activity, footage, and operator events. Mission Overwatch lets another user watch, log notes, and follow the mission timeline when that workflow is being used. After the mission, AlphaRelay helps the team upload footage, review what happened, generate a report, seal the record, and export an offline package.

## The basic flow

1. Start the mission from the Android field device.
2. Watch or log events in Mission Overwatch when needed.
3. End the mission when the field work is complete.
4. Upload and review footage.
5. Generate the after-action report.
6. Seal and export the final record.

## The two mission types

### Field Mission

Use this when the field device is the main tool and live local viewing is not required.

### Relay Mission

Use this when Mission Overwatch needs to connect to the field device for live viewing and command-side logging.
