This page is for field operators closing a mission and reviewers watching for upload readiness. Use it after flight to land, stop the mission, and confirm sync or queue state before deep review begins.

## Land the aircraft

On the pilot tablet or phone, tap **Land**, then follow normal landing and safety procedures.

When supported, AlphaRelay stops aircraft SD recording after a successful land command and prepares the mission video for handoff.

## Close the mission

Missions are always **started from the pilot Android workflow**. Closing can still happen from either side:

- **Pilot tablet or phone (recommended)** — tap **Stop Mission** to end the relay and close together. When pilot-handoff video is uploading, banners may prompt **Close mission** when safe.
- **Mission Overwatch** — open the **Mission** card and click **Close Mission** to finalize from the browser and drop any live relay session tied to overwatch focus.

Watch for queued footage handoffs on either device before final review begins.
