---

## Land the aircraft

On the pilot tablet, tap **Land**, then follow normal landing and safety procedures.

When supported, AlphaRelay may stop aircraft SD recording after a successful land command and prepare the mission video for handoff.

## Close the mission

In the Mission Console, go to the Mission card and click **Close Mission**. This ends the mission and drops the live relay session.

After closing, watch for any footage handoff or queued upload status on the pilot tablet and in Mission Overwatch before starting final review.
